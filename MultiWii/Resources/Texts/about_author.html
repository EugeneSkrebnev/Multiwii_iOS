My name is Eugene Skrebnev, I am from Belarus. I have been developing iOS applications for about 3 years, and also have good experience in competitive programming. My hobby is building and flying big and small quadrocopters, the Multiwii platform in particular. In February 2013 I was surprised to learn that there was no application to configure a Multiwii copter from an iOS device, so I decided to fill in the gap, and do it the best I can.

In May 2013 the idea materialised into a project which filled all my time free from university studies and my job, and took my money and other resources. I hope I have realised it almost as beautifully as possible, both in code and in the UI.

I am gaining good experience from it, developing the project from a mere idea, through sketches and beta versions, up to this AppStore publication. I would appreciate if you give any feedback, praise and critique, or buy the application, or help me to promote it.

If you have a project related to iOS and flying machines (or at least just a promising iOS project), I would be happy to join in.
