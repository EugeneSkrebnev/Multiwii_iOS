Version 1.0 Features:

  • Connecting your quadrocopter and your iOS device via the Bluetooth 4.0
  • Managing the main PID parameters
  • Managing the AUX channels
  • Saving PID parameters to the eprom.
  • Searching your copter by proximity.

Features available in the free version

The free version lets you test connection to the Multiwii PCB board, read the current PID parameters, calibrate the accelerometer and the magnetometer, and search your copter landing spot within 15-20 m (50-65 ft), with the Bluetooth transmitter as a beacon.

Limitations of the free version

You cannot write and save the PID parameters to your Multiwii PCB board.
